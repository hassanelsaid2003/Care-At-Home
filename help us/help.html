Medical Services
What medical services does Care At Home offer?
Care At Home provides a comprehensive range of telehealth and in-home services including online doctor consultations, medical diagnosis, tele-services, online prescriptions, men's and women's health programs, and certified home nursing visits. All services are available 24/7.
Can I get an online doctor consultation at any time?
Yes! Our online doctor service is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You can connect with a certified physician within minutes from your phone or computer — no waiting room needed.
Are your doctors licensed and certified in Egypt?
Absolutely. Every physician on our platform is fully licensed by the Egyptian Medical Syndicate and has been thoroughly vetted by our medical team. You can view each doctor's credentials, specialty, and patient reviews on their profile page.
Can I get an official doctor's note for work or school?
Yes. After your online consultation, your doctor can issue an official digital doctor's note which is stamped and legally valid. It will be sent directly to your email and also saved in your Care At Home patient profile.
Is Care At Home available outside Tanta?
Our online services (doctor consultations, prescriptions, tele-services) are available across all of Egypt. Physical home visits are currently available in Tanta and surrounding Gharbia Governorate cities, with expansion to more areas coming soon.
Pharmacy
How do I order medication from Care At Home pharmacy?
Browse our pharmacy page, add items to your cart, and checkout. You can pay via cash on delivery, Visa, Vodafone Cash, Fawry, or InstaPay. Orders placed before 8 PM are eligible for same-day delivery within Tanta.
Can I order prescription medication online?
Yes. Upload a photo of your valid prescription on our pharmacy page. Our licensed pharmacists review every prescription before dispensing. Alternatively, you can get an online prescription from one of our doctors in the same session.
What is the delivery time for pharmacy orders?
Orders placed before 8 PM are typically delivered the same day. Orders placed after 8 PM are delivered the next morning by 10 AM. Express delivery (within 2 hours) is available for an additional fee during peak hours.
Can I return a medication if it's wrong or damaged?
We offer a 30-day return policy for sealed, unopened medications. If you received the wrong product or a damaged item, contact us immediately via WhatsApp or our contact form and we'll arrange a free replacement or full refund within 24 hours.
Are pharmacy prices competitive with regular pharmacies?
Our prices match or beat the official Egyptian Pharmacy Authority prices. We also run regular flash sales, bundle deals, and loyalty discounts that make our prices even more competitive. No hidden charges — ever.
Appointments
How do I book an appointment with a doctor?
Visit our Doctors page, browse by specialty or name, and click "Book Appointment" on any doctor's profile. Choose your preferred date, time slot, and consultation type (online or home visit). You'll receive an SMS and email confirmation instantly.
Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment?
Yes. You can reschedule or cancel up to 2 hours before your appointment from your profile dashboard or by contacting support. Cancellations made at least 2 hours in advance receive a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.
How long does an online consultation take?
Most consultations take between 10–20 minutes. Complex cases or specialist consultations may take longer. You won't be rushed — our doctors give each patient the time they need to feel fully heard and properly treated.
What happens if my doctor is late to the appointment?
If your doctor is more than 10 minutes late, you have the option to wait or instantly switch to another available doctor at no extra cost. If you prefer, we'll refund your consultation fee in full, no questions asked.
Billing & Payments
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash on Delivery, Visa/Mastercard, Vodafone Cash, Fawry, and InstaPay. All online payment methods are secured with SSL encryption and comply with Egyptian Central Bank regulations.
Is my payment information stored securely?
We never store your full card details on our servers. All card transactions are processed through our PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We only retain a secure token for your convenience with future payments.
How do I get a refund?
Submit a refund request through your profile dashboard or our contact form. Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days back to your original payment method. Cash refunds are handled on the same delivery.
Do you offer any subscription or loyalty plans?
Yes! Our Care+ monthly subscription gives you unlimited online consultations, 15% off all pharmacy orders, priority home visit scheduling, and a dedicated health coordinator. Plans start at 299 EGP/month.
Home Visits
What home visit services are available?
We offer doctor home visits, home nursing care, physiotherapy sessions, IV drip therapy, blood sample collection for lab tests, post-operative wound care, and elderly care packages. All services are performed by certified professionals.
How quickly can a nurse or doctor arrive at my home?
Standard home visits are scheduled within 2–4 hours of booking. For urgent cases, our express home visit service guarantees arrival within 60 minutes in Tanta city. You'll receive live GPS tracking of your provider once dispatched.
Are home visit fees different from clinic fees?
Home visits include a small transport fee on top of the standard consultation fee. Exact pricing depends on your location and the type of service. You'll see the full cost breakdown before confirming your booking — no surprises.
Privacy & Security
Is my medical data kept confidential?
Your medical data is fully encrypted and stored on secure HIPAA-compliant servers. Only the healthcare providers involved in your care can access your records. We never share, sell, or disclose your health information to any third party.
Can I delete my account and all my data?
Yes. You have the right to request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Submit a deletion request from your profile settings or email privacy@careathome.com. We'll complete the deletion within 30 days and send you a confirmation.
Are online consultations recorded?
Consultations are never recorded without your explicit consent. If you choose to enable recording for your personal records (available in your session settings), recordings are encrypted and only accessible by you and your treating doctor.
Technical Support
The video call isn't working. What should I do?
First, check that your browser has camera and microphone permissions enabled. Try refreshing the page or switching to Chrome or Firefox for the best experience. If the issue persists, the consultation will automatically switch to a phone call. Our tech team is available 24/7 at support@careathome.com.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email or phone number and you'll receive a reset link via SMS and email within 2 minutes. The link is valid for 15 minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact support.
Which browsers and devices are supported?
Care At Home works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on desktop, tablet, and mobile. For the best video consultation experience, we recommend using Chrome or Firefox on a device with a front-facing camera and a stable internet connection (at least 3 Mbps).
Is there a mobile app available?
Our mobile app for Android and iOS is currently in development and will be launched soon. Until then, our website is fully responsive and works seamlessly on any smartphone browser — no app download needed.
