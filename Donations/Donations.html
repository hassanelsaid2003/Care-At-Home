🏥 Children's Cancer Hospital

hospital 57357

Hospital 57357 is a leading medical institution in Cairo, Egypt, specializing in the free treatment of childhood cancers, and is one of the largest children's hospitals in the world. Founded in 2007 through public donations, it boasts a 71.8% cure rate.
