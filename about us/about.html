about us

who are we?

Six students collaborated to develop a comprehensive medical website aimed at providing reliable health services and information to online users. The site includes a range of important sections such as definitions of various diseases, their symptoms, and treatment methods.

The website also includes a section for booking appointments with doctors and another for asking medical questions and receiving reliable answers, helping users communicate more effectively with specialists. Attention has also been given to providing up-to-date information.
