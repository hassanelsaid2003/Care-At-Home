Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.

How quickly will I receive a response?
Our support team typically responds within 2–4 hours during working hours. For urgent medical queries, our emergency line is available 24/7.
Can I book a medical consultation through this form?
Yes! Select "Appointment Booking" from the topic dropdown and describe your preferred time. Our coordinators will confirm your appointment within the same day.
Is my personal information kept confidential?
Absolutely. All information submitted through this form is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties. Please review our Privacy Policy for full details.
Do you offer home visit services?
Yes, Care At Home provides professional home visit services across Tanta and nearby areas. Select "Medical Consultation" and mention "home visit" in your message.
How can I track my pharmacy order?
After placing an order, you'll receive an SMS confirmation with a tracking link. You can also contact us via WhatsApp at +20 12447885544 for real-time updates.
