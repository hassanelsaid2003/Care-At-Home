Frequently Asked Questions❓

Answers to the most important user inquiries

Is the service really free?

Yes! All basic medical consultations and health follow-ups are completely free. There are some premium services (specialized visits, advanced scans) at symbolic prices less than 50% of traditional clinic prices.

How do you guarantee doctor quality?

All doctors on our platform are certified by the Egyptian Ministry of Health and Doctors Syndicate. We verify their certificates and practical experience. Each doctor is also evaluated after every visit to ensure continuous quality.

What areas do you cover?

We currently cover Greater Cairo, Alexandria, Tanta, Mansoura, and Zagazig governorates. Coming soon to all Egyptian governorates. You can check service availability in your area by entering your address in the app.

How do I book an appointment?

After logging in, click "Book Appointment", select the service type (consultation/home visit/lab tests), choose the required specialty, select your preferred date and time, then confirm the booking. You will receive an immediate confirmation message.

Can I cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment for free up to 4 hours before the scheduled time. To cancel: go to "My Appointments" and click cancel. No fees for early cancellation.

How do I contact technical support?

You can reach us via: Live chat in the app (fastest), Phone at 16600 (24/7), Email support@careathome.com, or through our social media pages. Average response time: 2 minutes for chat.
